Lecture 3 - HTML Nested Lists & Tables

Lists

Lists are of two types: ordered and unordered list.

Nested Lists Example

<ol>
  <li>Fruits
    <ul>
      <li>Apple</li>
      <li>Banana</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ol>

Output:

  1. Fruits
    • Apple
    • Banana
